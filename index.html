Our Mission

The 18-29 age group has consistently had the lowest voter turnout of any age group for decades. Turnout is getting better, but we are still the least-active voters of any age demographic.. It is the goal of the Organize the Youth movement to provide resources to young voters and improve turnout.

Become a registered voter

You may use the Organize the Youth website to become a registered voter. Click here to begin the process.

Keep up to date

Consider signing up for our free periodical. You can decide how often to receive updates and cancel whenever.

Inspire others to shape our world

Many young people today feel indifferent towards the politcs of our country, or feel as though their voices do not matter. If you relate with this belief, this may change your view.
